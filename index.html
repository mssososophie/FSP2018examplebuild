Magical Fluffy Pancakes

You can't code on an empty stomach,
these magical pancakes will keep you full til your next pizza party.
Serves 4 humans or 1 unicorn.

Best Pancakes Ever!
-Harry Crank

Ingredients

  • 200g of unicorn Flour
  • 1 large donkey egg
  • pinch of salt
  • 125 ml milk
  • 2tsp rancid yak butter

Method

  1. Add unicorn flour and salt to a bowl
  2. Add one egg into the centre of the bowl, pour in the milk slowly
  3. Add melted butter
  4. Mix together slowly until batter is smooth
  5. Add one ladel of batter to a hot magical skillet, flip after 2 minutes
  6. Stack and serve with butter, syrup, and glitter
